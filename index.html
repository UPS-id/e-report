About Us

E-Report is a digital platform designed to support reporting and monitoring various activities in schools, from academics to facilities. We are committed to providing the best service for comfort and progress together.

Services

E-Report provides academic, facility, and anonymous reporting services. Every report will be handled professionally and transparently by our team.

Academic Report

Report academic issues or needs, such as grades, schedules, or learning activities easily and quickly.

Facility Report

Submit complaints or requests for school facility repairs, such as classrooms, laboratories, or other public amenities.

Anonymous Report

Report incidents or problems anonymously to protect your privacy, such as bullying or other violations.
